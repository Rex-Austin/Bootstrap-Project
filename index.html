NIKE History

Nike's history began in 1964 as Blue Ribbon Sports, co-founded by Bill Bowerman and Phil Knight, initially distributing Japanese running shoes. In 1971, they rebranded as Nike, inspired by the Greek goddess of victory, and introduced the iconic Swoosh logo.
